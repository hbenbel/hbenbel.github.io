Experiences

Siemens Healthineers

I joined Siemens Healthineers from May 2019 to July 2020.
There I worked on Robust Left Heart Segmentation on 2D Ultrasound Images. My work was to research, development and help put in production a pipeline that segments heart chambers and then computes some metrics that are going to be used to assess the health of the patient. My work improved drastically the previous performances (up to 7 points in term of DICE score) and was subject of an Invention Disclosure. To accomplish this I used Python and PyTorch for model training/testing, data curation and automatic report generation and I used C++ to put into production the pipeline.
